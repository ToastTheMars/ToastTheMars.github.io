About Us

Bryan Mar

Bryan was born in Houston, Texas and even though he has spent the majority of his life in Fayetteville, he will always tell you he’s a Texan. Despite growing up in a burnt orange household he also decided to attended the University of Arkansas where he was once again reminded that hogs smell good. As fate would have it, staying in Fayetteville turned out to be one of the best decisions he would ever make.

Evan Harsh

Evan was born in Little Rock, Arkansas and has called many places home including Tampa, Florida and Denver, Colorado, but has called Fayetteville, AR home since she was in 5th grade. She stayed in Fayetteville to attend the University of Arkansas where she was a Chi Omega and has made her most steadfast friends and favorite memories in the city of Fayetteville.

Our Story

How We Met

Bryan and Evan first crossed each other’s paths in 1999 at Woodland Junior High School in the 7th grade band room. While they didn’t know each other at the time, they claim this was where they first met. It wasn’t until a Pre-AP Chemistry class during junior year at Fayetteville High School that Bryan and Evan truly became friends. Though they never dated in high school, their friendship continued to grow during senior year where they shared numerous classes and made many memories with each other and many of the friends you will see standing with them on their wedding day. Both Bryan and Evan attended college at the University of Arkansas, and though they had a handful of unintended run-ins it wasn’t until their senior year of college that fate stepped in. As luck would have it, Bryan and Evan lived down the street from one another—something that afforded them an opportunity to rekindle their friendship. By Christmas time of their senior year, Evan asked Bryan to attend her Chi Omega Semi-Formal, the Tacky Christmas Sweater Extravaganza, and the spark was ignited; the two began dating around the New Year and the rest is history. They have spent the past five years traveling together including 5 moving days, 10 vacations, 4 years of medical school, 1 precious puppy, 12 Razorback Football tailgates, countless date nights and one very special engagement.

Engagement

July 18th, 2013 Evan was scheduled to take the first part of her medical boards on July 17th. This was an 8-hour test that she had studied for all summer; even having to forgo vacations—including Bryan’s trip to Texas to celebrate his birthday. After finishing her test and driving home, she was greeted with beautiful flowers, a home cooked meal, and a very loving boyfriend. Sitting with those flowers was an envelope containing a few home made coupons. Bryan and Evan very commonly give each other home made coupons for celebrations—these could include things such as “One Free Dishes Washed Coupon,” “One Free Date Night Coupon” or Evan’s personal favorite: “One Free ‘You’re Right’ Coupon”. This set of coupons was different from others that Evan had received before; inside she found “One Free Mini-Vacation at the Crescent Hotel” and “One Free Swedish Massage at New Moon Day Spa”. Bryan and Evan had planned on going to Fayetteville a couple of days later, but Bryan had planned for them to leave the next morning and spend the night in Eureka Springs. The two checked into the hotel the next afternoon and spent some time exploring the haunted grounds before Evan continued on to her massage. After returning to the room, Evan noticed a bottle of champagne chilling and a gift bag on the table. After moving outside to open the champagne and take in the Ozark scenery, Bryan asked Evan to open her present. Beneath the mounds of paper she caught a glimpse of a small blue box tied in a white ribbon and immediately began to cry. She pulled out the box and, for a moment, thought that it was a cruel joke, as it felt empty. However, when she opened the box she found a piece of paper folded up with one more coupon—“One Free Happy Life”. When Evan looked up from the sheet of paper, she saw Bryan down on one knee, holding a beautiful ring in his hand. Through tears and laughter, Evan said yes and the day became another part of their history together. Evan was completely surprised because she always knew that Bryan wanted to ask her parent’s blessing but didn’t know when he did this. Little did she know, Bryan’s trip back to Texas two weeks earlier had been cut short so he could make a pit stop in Fayetteville on his way back to Corinth; something she discovered once Bryan showed her the engagement video below.
